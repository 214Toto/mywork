Renal Pathology
Glomerular Diseases

What term is used for diseases that primarily involve the renal glomeruli.
✓ Glomerulonephritis (GN) or Bright’s disease
Name the groups that glomerular disease are grouped as.
✓ Primary Glomerulonephritis
✓ Secondary Glomerulonephritis
Discuss the Clinicopathologic Classification of Glomerular Diseases
✓ These are seen from the concept of Primary and Secondary GN.
✓ Primary is where we have Acute GN, Chronic GN, and Rapidly Progressive GN among others.
✓ Secondary is where we have Systemic Diseases like Lupus nephritis (SLE), Hereditary Diseases like Goodpasture’s syndrome.
